About FFI

FFI is a career consulting firm, assisting in B2C immigration. We provide professional visa services and immigration consultancy solutions. Our service gives you a hassle free immigration process for all categories like students, employees, skilled workers and investors. We have a panel of consultants who are experienced lawyers, counselors related to Canadian immigration. Our review team will review your application personally and process it within seven days.

Our Mission

To deliver foreign migrants, a quality Canadian immigration experiences.
