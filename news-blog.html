Unlike the US, Canada continues to operate their immigration system!!

In order to protect the jobs of Americans, the U.S. President has temporarily suspended the PR applications made by those outside of the United States for 60 days. This period of suspension has the possibility of extensions with longer term impacts, while it has some exceptions.

But, in Canada it's not the case. Canada is not going to adapt to similar layoff methods as it recognizes the importance of immigration on to the labor market and the country's economy. In fact, the country welcomes three times as many immigrants on a per capita basis than it does.

Though there are some restrictions on the foreign travel which are completely temporary as a measure to stop the spread of corona, the government is still approving the Canadian PR applications. Provinces and Territories in Canada are now enabled to have partnership with the Federal government to take care of their own immigration programs which can benefit their social and economical objectives.

To support Canadian key industries, the country considers immigration to be the best factor to upscale economic, demographic and social prospects.
